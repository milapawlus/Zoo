HAVE YOU EVER BEEN CLOSE TO PANDA?

You experience that during the day You start with a short tour on our way down to the panda. Here, a Zooguide will tell exciting stories about the pandas and their beautiful facility.   • Price: SEK 495
  • Time: At 11:00 - 12:15
  • Age limit: Min. 5 years
  • Number of participants: Max. 10 people
