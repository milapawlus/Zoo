Welcome to the GARDEN!

EXPLORE our botanical gardens during your zoo visit and discover our passion for plants that not only makes Animal Nation look beautiful but also helps to provide important habitats for native wildlife.

Animal nation is home to three National Plant Collections – including the nepenthes collection. This species of plant, also known as a tropical pitcher plant or monkey cup, can be found across South East Asia and has a special diet for a plant…they’re flesh eaters!

Meet out three National Plant Collections

Cactus

Our collection of cactus at the zoo is hugeand amazing! They’re mesmerising and many also bloom big, beautiful, fantastic and colourful flowers.

Nepenthes

Our National Plant Collection of Nepenthes holds over 2500 individual plants from 130 species and countless hybrids, which are facing extinction.

Orchids

With over 10,000 orchid plants, Animal Nation has one of the largest orchid collections in the Sweden. The collection contains over 3,600 unique varieties.
