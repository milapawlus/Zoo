About our Zoo

Zoo Animal Nation is managed by the Swedish Zoological Society, a non-governmental organization established to create the first local zoo for Swedes. Animal Nation was officially opened on 14th November 1963 and has matured into a well-known zoo all around the world. We have a total of over 5137 specimen from 476 species of mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians and fish. We also have a garedn with three collections of plants.

Zoo Animal Nation covers 110 acres of land which is situated only 5km from the city of Stockhol. Over the years, the zoo has transformed itself to an open concept zoo with over 90% of its animals being kept in spacious exhibits with landscape befitting its nature. We are working in making sure that the old zoo concept is changed entirely.
