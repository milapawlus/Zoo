Cactus Collection

With over 10,000 cactus plants, Animal Nation has one of the largest cactus collections in the Sweden. The collection contains over 3,600 unique varieties, representing 280 genera, including, but not limited to, Easter cactus, Old lady cactus, Bunny ear cactus, Blue columnar cactus, Star cactus and Barrel cactus.
